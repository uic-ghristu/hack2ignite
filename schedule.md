Event Format
The hackathon will be conducted in a three-round format. The first two rounds will be conducted online and the final round will be on-campus offline. The registration will be hosted on Unstop Platform.
Round 0: Registration Phase
Duration: 15+ days
Open registration for participants across the country.
Platform - unstop.com
Round 1: Qualifier Round
Duration: 48 hours
Registered teams will receive the problem statement after the registration phase.
Teams must submit a prototype and PPT within the given time.
Round 2: On-Campus Offline Pitch Round
Conducted 15 days after Round 1
Duration: 12 hours
The top 20 teams, shortlisted on the basis of their prototype and PPT submissions, will advance to the final round.
Shortlisted teams will pitch their solutions and present their prototypes before the judging panel.
